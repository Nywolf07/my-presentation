The hand nobody shook

The dedicated follow-up to the 211 Week-3 hand-raisers
The headline finding. 95% opened, 77% clicked "Talk to an Expert" — then 0 booked.
Why zero — and why it isn't failure:
• Scoring model literally shows "Calendar / Form Fill — none yet".
• Phone capture stripped for DPO / compliance.
• WhatsApp provisioned but never switched on (0 points).
• Human sales hand-off still being prepared when the window closed.
What the pilot proved:
Demand generation and qualification work at scale — hundreds of repeat hand-raisers and a 30-lead sales-ready dossier. Conversion is the un-run next phase, not a failed one.
